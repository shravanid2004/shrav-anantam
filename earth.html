Earth: The only planet to support life.

Earth is the third planet from the Sun and the only known celestial body to harbor life. With a diverse range of ecosystems, Earth supports millions of species, including humans. The planet's unique position in the "Goldilocks zone" allows it to maintain temperatures that sustain liquid water, essential for life as we know it. Earth has a rich atmosphere composed of nitrogen (about 78%) and oxygen (about 21%), which protects life from harmful solar radiation and provides essential gases for respiration and photosynthesis. The planet's surface is composed of approximately 71% water, with oceans, rivers, and lakes playing a critical role in regulating climate and supporting biodiversity. Earth’s dynamic geology is marked by tectonic activity, mountains, and varied landscapes, making it a unique and constantly changing planet in the solar system.
