Solar Flares Solar flares are intense bursts of radiation caused by the release of magnetic energy from the Sun. These flares can emit high-energy particles and electromagnetic radiation that can affect satellites, power grids, and communication systems on Earth. A massive solar flare hitting the Earth could disrupt the electrical infrastructure, potentially leading to widespread blackouts, which could be fatal in situations dependent on technology and power. Additionally, astronauts in space could be exposed to harmful radiation during solar events.
