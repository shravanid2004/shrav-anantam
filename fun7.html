MIND WOBBLING

The Fermi Paradox The Fermi Paradox poses a haunting question: If the universe is so vast and old, and there are potentially millions of habitable planets, where is everybody? Given the vast number of stars and planets, we should have encountered extraterrestrial life by now, yet there is no concrete evidence of it. This paradox leads to various chilling theories, including the possibility that advanced civilizations self-destruct, or that they are deliberately avoiding us.
