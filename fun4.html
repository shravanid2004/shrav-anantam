Cosmic Rays Cosmic rays are high-energy particles from outer space that can penetrate Earth's atmosphere. While our atmosphere and magnetic field protect us from most of these particles, exposure to high levels can be harmful. Cosmic rays can damage DNA and lead to an increased risk of cancer, especially for astronauts or individuals traveling at high altitudes. Prolonged exposure to cosmic rays in space is a significant concern for long-term human space travel, such as missions to Mars.
